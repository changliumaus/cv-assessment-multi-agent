Senior Backend Engineer

Company: InnovateTech Solutions
Location: San Francisco, CA (Hybrid)
Employment Type: Full-time
Department: Engineering

ABOUT US
InnovateTech Solutions is a fast-growing fintech company revolutionizing digital payments.
We process billions of transactions annually and are looking for talented engineers to join
our mission of making financial services accessible to everyone.

JOB DESCRIPTION
We are seeking an experienced Senior Backend Engineer to join our Platform Engineering team.
You will be responsible for designing and building highly scalable, reliable backend systems
that power our payment processing infrastructure. This role requires strong technical skills,
leadership abilities, and a passion for solving complex distributed systems challenges.

KEY RESPONSIBILITIES
• Design, develop, and maintain scalable backend services and APIs
• Build and optimize high-throughput payment processing systems
• Collaborate with cross-functional teams including Product, Frontend, and DevOps
• Lead technical design discussions and architecture reviews
• Mentor junior and mid-level engineers
• Participate in on-call rotation for production support
• Write clean, maintainable, and well-tested code
• Contribute to technical documentation and best practices

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
• 5+ years of professional software engineering experience
• Strong proficiency in Python or Go
• Experience with web frameworks (Django, Flask, FastAPI, or similar)
• Deep understanding of RESTful API design and microservices architecture
• Solid experience with SQL databases (PostgreSQL, MySQL) and query optimization
• Experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
• Strong understanding of distributed systems concepts
• Experience with containerization (Docker) and orchestration (Kubernetes)
• Excellent problem-solving and debugging skills
• Strong communication and collaboration abilities
• Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or equivalent experience

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
• Experience in fintech or payment processing systems
• Knowledge of event-driven architectures and message queues (Kafka, RabbitMQ)
• Experience with NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Redis)
• Familiarity with monitoring and observability tools (Prometheus, Grafana, DataDog)
• Experience with infrastructure as code (Terraform, CloudFormation)
• Contributions to open source projects
• Experience with GraphQL
• Understanding of security best practices and compliance requirements (PCI-DSS)

TECHNICAL STACK
• Backend: Python, Go, Django, FastAPI
• Databases: PostgreSQL, Redis, MongoDB
• Infrastructure: AWS, Kubernetes, Docker, Terraform
• CI/CD: GitHub Actions, Jenkins
• Monitoring: Prometheus, Grafana, DataDog
